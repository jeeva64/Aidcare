About Our Platform

Virtual Donation Board is a platform designed to facilitate the donation of items directly to those in need. Whether it's old books, clothes, or toys, your contributions can make a significant difference.

Join hands with us to support trusts and orphanages within your community. Track your inventory, browse donation requests, and make a difference at your convenience.
